     1 # Design Overhaul Handoff

       3 ## What We Did
       4
       5 **Goal:** Full visual redesign of IndyHackers Redux based
         on a "Civic Monospace" spec — yellow canvas, white cards,
         Space Mono headings/buttons/nav, Space Grotesk body text,
         warm design tokens throughout.
       6
       7 **Stack:** Vue 3 + Bootstrap Vue Next + SCSS. No Tailwind.
          All styling via CSS custom properties.
       8
       9 **Branch:** `design-spike` (all work here, not merged to m
         ain)
      10
      11 ---
      12
      13 ## Key Architecture Decisions
      14
      15 1. **CSS token system** in `src/assets/base.scss`:
      16    - `--background: #FFCC00` (yellow canvas)
      17    - `--foreground: #121212` (near-black text)
      18    - `--primary: #121212` / `--primary-foreground: #FFCC00
         ` (button pair)
      19    - `--card: #FFFFFF` (white cards)
      20    - `--border: #CFC4A8` (warm border)
      21    - `--muted-foreground: #595959`
      22
      23 2. **Padding rule:** `.ih-container` owns horizontal paddi
         ng (`0 3rem`), outer elements own vertical only — never st
         ack both or you get double gutters.
      24
      25 3. **`main.scss` must be imported in `App.vue`** — it hold
         s `.ih-btn-primary`, `.ih-btn-outline`, and `.ih-container
         `. Was missing early on and caused buttons to render as un
         styled links.
      26
      27 4. **`SponsorsView` lives in `src/views/`** not `src/compo
         nents/` — router import was updated accordingly.
      28
      29 5. **Fonts:** Space Mono (400, 700) for headings/nav/butto
         ns; Space Grotesk (300–700) for body. Imported in `App.vue
         `. Dosis and Work Sans removed.
